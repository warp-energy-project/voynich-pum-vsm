Model

The PUM/VSM model treats the Voynich text as a functional system. It does not claim a final linguistic decipherment. It maps token patterns into structural roles such as state, operation, carrier, result, and termination.

Author / Research

Research by Tomasz Grabowski (Drako). Analytical support using AI tools. The model was developed through iterative testing of image-text coupling, Y-axis dependency, local echo, and token-role mapping.
