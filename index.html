Model Explanation

The PUM/VSM model treats Voynich text not as ordinary natural language, but as a parametric and procedural system.

Core relation: IMAGE → PARAMETERS → TEXT DYNAMICS.

The visual structure of the folio may influence textual behaviour through vertical position, image geometry, and local echo patterns.
