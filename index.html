Human–AI Collaboration: Research Origin

This project did not start as a typical software idea. It emerged from a long-term collaboration between a human researcher and multiple AI systems, evolving step by step into a structured research environment.

  • Drako: intuition, direction, pattern recognition
  • Navi: structure, logic, system building
  • Brat: validation, expansion, pressure-testing
AI becomes most effective not when replacing human thinking, but when working with a human who is actively engaged, open, and willing to explore non-standard paths.
